XR is an umbrella term for experiences that combine the physical and digital
worlds at different degrees of immersion.

In simple terms:

- **Augmented reality** adds digital layers to the physical world.
- **Virtual reality** replaces the environment around you with a digital one.
- **Mixed reality** allows digital and physical elements to coexist with stronger spatial awareness.

The goal of this note is not to lock down final definitions, but to create a
base for exploring how these technologies appear in products, research,
education, industry, art, and entertainment.
