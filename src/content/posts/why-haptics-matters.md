Haptics is the set of technologies that use tactile feedback to communicate
sensations through the body. In XR, this can appear in controllers, gloves,
vests, surfaces, vibration, resistance, or simulated touch.

The interest in haptics comes from a central tension in immersive experiences:
we see and hear digital worlds with increasing detail, but we still touch very
little inside them.

When following this area, it is worth looking not only at the technical effect,
but also at the use context. Simple feedback may be enough for games or
training, while medical, industrial, or collaborative simulations may require
more precise responses.
